# Irreversible Encryption

You're a security analyst investigating suspicious activity from a colleague who calls himself "The Encryption Master."

## Security Incident Report

```
INTERNAL MEMO - SECURITY TEAM

Subject: SUSPICIOUS ENCRYPTION ACTIVITY

INCIDENT SUMMARY:
Our colleague has been sending encrypted messages to an unknown contact
he calls his "BESTO FRIENDO." He claims his encryption is so efficient
that from a short text it creates a huuuuuuuuge file - and that it's
completely irreversible.

RECOVERED EVIDENCE:
- irreversible_encryptor.py: His custom encryption script
- encrypted_flag.txt: The encrypted message

YOUR MISSION:
Reverse the encryption and recover the original message.

Good luck, analyst.
```
